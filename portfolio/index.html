Introduction

My name is Ndifreke Ndue, I've built a career that uniquely blends the world of music and technology. I have maintained my technical edge through freelance support work individuals and small businesses, helping to troubleshoot systems and software and proffer solutions accordingly. I am now channeling my passion, diverse skillset and disciplined approach to problem solving into a full-time focus on backend engineering.
